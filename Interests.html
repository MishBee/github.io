My interests in information technology are software development, robotics, and VR (Virtual Reality).VR and games development.
I am also interested in the hardware aspect, the ability to piece together different pieces of technology to create a pseudo living system
to enhance one's surroundings. I would ideally like to enter a career into software development as I have been learning programming as a hobby.
My aim is to complete a Bachelor’s in Information Technology or Computer Science.

Information Technology piqued my interest in my early teen years, initially from playing games on a Sega MegaDrive,
moving up through various consoles. After a lengthy duration, I felt some of the games I would play were lacking in
certain features, mechanics, gameplay. This in turn, led to an interest in game development, thinking to myself that
I could one day start developing my own games with the features added that I wished were in others.

This era of my life was the start of my learning journey into Information Technology.

My experience with Information Technology has included software development as a hobby, which I am still learning.
I have also carried out multiple personal computers builds which I have succeeded with, with one being quite recent,
which was required for the study of this course (Introduction to Information Technology). Software development languages
that I have had experience with include C++, C#, minor experience with HTML (HyperText Markup Language), CSS, JavaScript,
and I am currently learning Python to add to my repertoire.

Throughout the years, troubleshooting and problem-solving various pieces of technology have been included in my experience.
My reasoning for studying with RMIT (Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology) was the course outline and structure provided,
which included the study of various fields I was interested in. Another reason for my choice, was the access to a degree
without the standard procedures for acceptance, i.e.: Atar score, previous relevant field study.

Adding access to networking opportunities with industry leaders tutoring and course content provided by people who
have had the relevant experience within the industry to cater for current demands.

My expectations for the learning outcomes in this course are to build on previous skills, learn new skills
and knowledge that are relevant to the Information Technology industry. The eventual goal is to establish and
maintain a career in the Information Technology field. Through studying this course, I believe I will gain
knowledge over a broad range of subjects, allowing me to discern a future career path while learning the
relevant skills necessary to achieve that goal.
